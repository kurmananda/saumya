Bio

Passionate Machine Learning/Deep Learning enthusiast with 2.5 years of hands-on experience in data analysis . Skilled at transforming raw data into impactful insights and building machine learning models that drive smarter decisions. With a strong foundation in statistical analysis, data visualization, and predictive modeling, I’m always eager to push the boundaries of what’s possible with data. I thrive on solving complex challenges and continuously learning to stay at the forefront of machine learning and deep learning advancements.
